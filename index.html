Welcome to Diagrid

At Diagrid, we specialize in delivering efficient, safe, and innovative structural solutions tailored to meet the needs of modern Construction. Our team includes certified Professional Engineers (P.E.), ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of safety, quality, and code compliance. We are committed to supporting projects from concept through completion with precision, professionalism, and client-focused service.

Our Services

  • Structural Calculations Accurate and code-compliant load calculations for all structural elements, ensuring stability, safety, and efficiency in every design.
  • Structural Analysis & Design Advanced analysis and detailed design of Concrete, Steel, Wood structure using state-of-the art software and engineering principles for both new construction and renovations.
  • 3D Rendering High-quality, photorealistic visualizations that bring your concepts to life. Our 3D services include:
    Interior Design Renderings – Realistic layouts, material finishes, and lighting concepts.
    Elevation Design – Detailed exterior façade visuals for both modern and traditional styles.
    Exterior & Interior Walkthroughs – Immersive animated tours that help clients visualize the space before construction begins.
  • Cost Estimation Reliable and detailed cost projections to assist in budgeting, planning, and decision-making throughout the design and construction process.
